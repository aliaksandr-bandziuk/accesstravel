Eiffel Tower Tours and Tickets

5,556 Отзывов

The Eiffel Tower isn't just a symbol of Paris but a symbol for all of France. Erected by Gustave Eiffel to commemorate the centennial of the French Revolution in 1889, the 1,050-foot (320-meter) tower once held the title of the world's tallest structure. Despite having been dwarfed by Dubai's Burj Khalifa and The Shard in London, the Eiffel Tower remains one of the most recognizable landmarks on the planet. View the architectural icon from afar, or stop in at the three observation levels for stellar city views.

Overview

The Eiffel Tower is one of Paris' most popular attractions — why not bypass those lengthy lines all the way up to the second floor? This small-group, skip the ticket desk line gets you entry to the base of the Eiffel Tower and direct access to the tower's second-level viewing platform, which offers views of everything from the Louvre Museum to Notre Dame Cathedral. A guide accompanies you, providing details on the tower's origins and intricacies as you go.

  • Small-group tour limited to 20 people ensures personal attention from the guide.
